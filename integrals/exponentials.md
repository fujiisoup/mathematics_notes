# Exponential
$$
\int_0^\infty \exp\left(-\alpha x\right) dx = \frac{1}{\alpha}
$$

## Polynomial and exponential
### 1st order
$$
\int_0^\infty x \exp\left(-\alpha x\right) dx = \frac{1}{\alpha^2}
$$

### 2nd order
$$
\int_0^\infty x^2 \exp\left(-\alpha x\right) dx = \frac{2}{\alpha^3}
